<p><p>Blowers are categorized into three main market types: low pressure, medium pressure, and high pressure. Low pressure blowers are designed for applications requiring lower air flow and pressure, such as ventilation and dust collection systems. Medium pressure blowers are used for applications that require moderate air flow and pressure, like pneumatic conveying and drying systems. High pressure blowers are utilized for applications that demand high air flow and pressure, such as combustion air supply and air scouring in wastewater treatment plants.</p></p>
