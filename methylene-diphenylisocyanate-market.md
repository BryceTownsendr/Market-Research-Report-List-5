<p><p>Methylene Diphenylisocyanate, or MDI, is divided into three main market types: Polymeric MDI, Pure MDI, and Modified MDI. Polymeric MDI is a mixture of MDI isocyanates with higher molecular weight and viscosity, often used in rigid foam applications. Pure MDI contains a higher percentage of MDI and is used in flexible foam production. Modified MDI is MDI that has been modified with other compounds to improve certain properties, such as moisture resistance or reactivity. Each type serves different purposes in various industries.</p></p>
