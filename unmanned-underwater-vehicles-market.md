<p><p>Unmanned Underwater Vehicles (UUVs) market consists of two main types: Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs) and Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs). AUVs are self-propelled vehicles that operate independently without human intervention, collecting data and performing tasks such as mapping the ocean floor. ROVs, on the other hand, are controlled by an operator from the surface and are primarily used for tasks that require precision and manipulation, such as underwater construction and maintenance. Both AUVs and ROVs play important roles in various industries, including oil and gas exploration, marine research, and military operations.</p></p>
