<p><strong>The Car Fleet Leasing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Close End Lease</li><li>Open End Lease</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Close end lease and open end lease are two types of car fleet leasing market options. In a close end lease, the lessee returns the vehicle at the end of the lease term and is not responsible for any additional costs beyond normal wear and tear. In an open end lease, the lessee is responsible for any difference between the residual value and the actual value of the vehicle at the end of the lease term. Close end leases are more common and provide more predictability for the lessee, while open end leases carry more risk but also potential rewards.</p></p>
