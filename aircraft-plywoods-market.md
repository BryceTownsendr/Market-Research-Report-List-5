<p><p>Aircraft plywoods are categorized into three main market types based on thickness: below 6mm, 6mm-18mm, and above 18mm. Below 6mm aircraft plywoods are typically used for lightweight applications such as interior paneling and decorative purposes. Plywoods within the 6mm-18mm range are commonly used for structural components like ribs, spars, and bulkheads. Aircraft plywoods above 18mm are utilized for heavy-duty applications such as wing spars and landing gear components. Each market type serves a specific purpose in the aviation industry, providing strength and durability in different aircraft structures.</p></p>
