<p><strong>The LCD Color Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CSTN-LCD Filters</li><li>TFT-LCD Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CSTN-LCD filters refer to Color Super Twisted Nematic LCD filters, which are commonly used in older generation LCD screens. These filters provide good color reproduction and are cost-effective. On the other hand, TFT-LCD filters stand for Thin-Film Transistor LCD filters, which are commonly found in modern LCD screens. TFT-LCD filters offer higher resolution, faster response times, and better image quality compared to CSTN-LCD filters. Both types of filters play a crucial role in enhancing the color display and overall performance of LCD screens.</p></p>
