<p><p>RF GaN technology has evolved to offer three main market types: Gan-On-Sic, Gan-On-Silicon, and Gan-On-Diamond. Gan-On-Sic is known for its high power density and thermal conductivity, making it suitable for high-frequency applications. Gan-On-Silicon offers a cost-effective solution for medium to high-power RF devices. Gan-On-Diamond provides exceptional thermal management and is ideal for high-power, high-frequency applications. Each market type caters to different needs and requirements, offering a range of benefits such as power density, cost-effectiveness, and thermal conductivity.</p></p>
