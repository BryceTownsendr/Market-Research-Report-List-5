<p><p>Industrial Solid Bowl Centrifuge (SBC) market is categorized into two types: Two-Phase Solid Bowl Centrifuge and Three-Phase Solid Bowl Centrifuge. Two-Phase SBC is used for separating two immiscible liquids with different densities, such as oil and water. On the other hand, Three-Phase SBC is used for separating three different phases, such as oil, water, and solids. Both types of SBC are utilized in industries like oil and gas, chemical, and food processing for various separation and clarification processes.</p></p>
