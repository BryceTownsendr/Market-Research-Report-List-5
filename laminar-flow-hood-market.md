<p><p>Laminar Flow Hoods are used in laboratories to provide a clean and sterile work environment. There are two main types of Laminar Flow Hoods: Horizontal and Vertical. </p><p>Horizontal Laminar Flow Hoods have airflow moving parallel to the work surface, while Vertical Laminar Flow Hoods have airflow moving from top to bottom. Horizontal hoods are often used for applications such as tissue culture and electronics assembly, while vertical hoods are commonly used for chemical fume containment and sterile compounding. Both types of hoods help maintain a controlled environment for sensitive experiments and procedures.</p></p>
