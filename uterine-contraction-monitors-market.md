<p><p>Uterine contraction monitors are devices used to measure and monitor contractions during labor. There are two main types of uterine contraction monitors: Internal monitors, which are inserted into the uterus to directly measure contractions, and External monitors, which are placed on the abdomen to measure contractions externally. Internal monitors provide more accurate readings but are invasive, while external monitors are non-invasive but may not provide as precise measurements. Both types play a crucial role in monitoring the progress of labor and ensuring the safety of both the mother and the baby.</p></p>
