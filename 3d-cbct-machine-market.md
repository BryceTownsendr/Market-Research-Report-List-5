<p><p>3D CBCT machines are available in different market types, such as large FOV and medium FOV. Large FOV machines have a wider field of view, making them suitable for comprehensive imaging of the entire jaw and skull. They are commonly used in oral surgery and orthodontics. On the other hand, medium FOV machines have a smaller field of view, ideal for imaging specific areas like individual teeth or sinuses. These machines are commonly used in general dentistry for diagnostics and treatment planning.</p></p>
