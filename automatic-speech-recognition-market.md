<p><ul><li>Speaker-Dependent Speech Recognition System</li><li>Speaker-Independent Speech System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) market includes two main types: Speaker-Dependent Speech Recognition System and Speaker-Independent Speech Recognition System. Speaker-Dependent systems require users to train the system by providing samples of their speech in order to accurately recognize their voice. On the other hand, Speaker-Independent systems do not require training and can recognize speech from any user. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications such as virtual assistants, voice-controlled devices, and speech-to-text technologies.</p></p>
