<p><p>Velocity type compressors are categorized into three main types: Axial Compressor, Centrifugal Compressor, and Francis Compressor. Axial compressors have a series of rotating blades that increase the velocity of the air, while centrifugal compressors use a high-speed rotating impeller to increase air pressure. Francis compressors combine elements of both axial and centrifugal compressors, utilizing both radial and axial flow. Each type of compressor has its own advantages and applications based on factors such as size, efficiency, and pressure requirements.</p></p>
