<p><p>Plunger sensors come in various types including 2-wire sensors, 3-wire sensors, and rattle sensors. 2-wire sensors are simple and cost-effective, requiring only two wires to connect to a control system. 3-wire sensors offer increased accuracy and stability by using an additional wire for reference. Rattle sensors are designed to detect changes in position or movement and are commonly used in automotive applications.
