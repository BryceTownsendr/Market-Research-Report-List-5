<p><p>Corrugated paperboard is categorized into different types based on the number of layers it contains. Single wall paperboard has one layer of corrugated medium between two layers of linerboard. Double wall paperboard consists of two layers of corrugated medium and three layers of linerboard. Triple wall paperboard has three layers of corrugated medium and four layers of linerboard. Other types of corrugated paperboard may include specialty boards with unique compositions or additional layers for specific packaging requirements.</p></p>
