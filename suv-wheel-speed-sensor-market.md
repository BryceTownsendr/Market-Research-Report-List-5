<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SUV Wheel Speed Sensor market includes two main types - Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type sensors use a Hall Effect sensor to detect the magnetic field created by the rotating wheel. On the other hand, Magnetic Electric Type sensors utilize a magnet and coil setup to generate an electric signal based on wheel speed. Both types offer accurate detection of wheel speed, enabling advanced safety features like ABS and traction control in SUVs. These sensors play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient driving experiences.</p></p>
