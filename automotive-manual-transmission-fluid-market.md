<p><p>Manual transmission fluid is classified into different types based on their additives and properties, designated as GL-1 to GL-5. GL-1 to GL-3 fluids are basic formulations suitable for older manual transmissions, while GL-4 is designed for modern synchronized transmissions. GL-5 fluids have additional additives for enhanced protection against wear and extreme pressure. These different types cater to varying transmission requirements and ensure smooth operation, efficient lubrication, and longevity of manual transmission systems in vehicles.</p></p>
