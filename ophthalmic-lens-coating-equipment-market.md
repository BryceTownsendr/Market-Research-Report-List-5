<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Lens Coating Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dip Coating</li><li>Spin Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ophthalmic lens coating equipment market includes two main types of coating processes: Dip coating and Spin coating. Dip coating involves immersing the lens in a liquid coating material and then allowing it to dry, creating a uniform coating. Spin coating, on the other hand, involves spinning the lens at high speeds while applying the coating material, resulting in a thin and even layer. Both methods are commonly used in the ophthalmic industry to enhance the durability, clarity, and functionality of lenses.</p></p>
