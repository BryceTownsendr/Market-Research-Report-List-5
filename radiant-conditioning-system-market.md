<p><p>Radiant conditioning systems consist of two main market types: water heating and electric heating. Water heating systems use heated water running through tubing beneath the floor to provide warmth and are typically more energy-efficient. On the other hand, electric heating systems use electric cables or mats to generate heat and can be easier to install in existing buildings. Both types of radiant conditioning systems offer the benefits of even heat distribution, improved energy efficiency, and customizable temperature control for a comfortable indoor environment.</p></p>
