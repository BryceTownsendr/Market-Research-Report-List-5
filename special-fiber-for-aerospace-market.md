<p><p>Special fibers for the aerospace industry can be categorized into two main types: conductive and insulation fibers. Conductive fibers are designed to dissipate electrical charges and prevent static buildup, which is crucial for protecting sensitive electronic equipment in aircraft. On the other hand, insulation fibers are used to provide thermal protection and fire resistance, helping to maintain a safe and controlled environment in aerospace applications. Both types of fibers play a vital role in ensuring the reliability and performance of aerospace systems.</p></p>
