<p><p>Sterility testing market includes different methods such as direct inoculation, membrane filtration, and others. Direct inoculation involves directly inoculating the sample into growth media to detect microbial growth. Membrane filtration involves passing the sample through a membrane filter to trap any microorganisms, which are then cultured on agar plates. Other methods in the market may include rapid methods such as polymerase chain reaction (PCR) or alternative culture-based techniques. These methods are essential in ensuring the sterility of pharmaceutical products and medical devices.</p></p>
