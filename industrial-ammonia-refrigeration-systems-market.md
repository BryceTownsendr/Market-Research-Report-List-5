<p><p>Industrial ammonia refrigeration systems market consists of several types of equipment including evaporator units, industrial racks, compressors, and heat exchangers. Evaporator units are responsible for cooling the ammonia gas back to a liquid state. Industrial racks house all the components of the system and control the flow of ammonia throughout the system. Compressors are used to compress the ammonia gas for circulation. Heat exchangers transfer heat between the ammonia gas and the external environment to cool the system.</p></p>
