<p><p>Dynamic Material Testing Machine market is divided into two main types: electromechanical type and hydraulic type. The electromechanical type machines use an electric motor to drive the load frame and apply force to the sample under test. These machines are ideal for high-speed testing and have a wide range of force capacities. On the other hand, hydraulic type machines use hydraulic fluid to apply force to the sample, offering precise control and high force capacities. Both types cater to different testing needs and applications in material testing industry.</p></p>
