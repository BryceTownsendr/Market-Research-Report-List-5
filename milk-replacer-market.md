<p><p>Milk replacer market types can be categorized into whey-based and skim-based products. Whey-based milk replacers are made from the liquid byproduct of cheese production, which contains high levels of protein and essential amino acids. Skim-based milk replacers are made from skim milk powder, which is low in fat content but still provides protein and other nutrients. Both types of milk replacers are commonly used in livestock feeding to supplement or replace maternal milk for young animals.</p></p>
