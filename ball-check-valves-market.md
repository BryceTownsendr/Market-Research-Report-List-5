<p><p>Ball check valves are mechanical devices that allow fluid to flow in one direction while preventing backflow in the opposite direction. These valves consist of a ball that sits within a seat, allowing fluid to pass through when the pressure in the pipeline is greater on one side of the valve. When the pressure on the opposite side is greater, the ball is forced against the seat, preventing backflow.</p><p>The Ball Check Valves Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.5% during the forecast period. The market growth is driven by the increasing demand for ball check valves in various industries such as oil & gas, water & wastewater, chemical, and food & beverages.
