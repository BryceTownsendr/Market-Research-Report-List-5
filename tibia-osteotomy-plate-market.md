<p><p>Tibia osteotomy plates are devices used in orthopedic surgeries to treat conditions like bow legs or knock knees by correcting the alignment of the tibia bone. These plates are available in two main types based on the screw diameter - 4.5 mm and 5.0 mm. The 4.5 mm plates are smaller and lighter, suitable for patients with less severe deformities, while the 5.0 mm plates are stronger and used for more complex cases. Both types offer surgeons options to choose the most appropriate plate for each patient's specific needs.</p></p>
