<p><p>The 4K Smart Set Top Box (STB) market is divided into five main types: IPTV, Satellite, DTT (Digital Terrestrial Television), OTT (Over-The-Top), and Hybrid. IPTV STBs receive TV content through internet protocols, while Satellite STBs receive content via satellite signals. DTT STBs receive signals through terrestrial antennas, while OTT STBs stream content over the internet. Hybrid STBs combine multiple technologies to offer a wider range of content options to consumers. Each market type offers unique features and benefits to cater to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
