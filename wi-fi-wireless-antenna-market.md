<p><p>Wi-Fi Wireless Antenna market consists of two main types: Omni directional antennas and Directional antennas. Omni directional antennas emit signals in all directions, making them suitable for providing coverage in all directions. They are commonly used in settings where signals need to reach multiple devices in different areas. Directional antennas, on the other hand, focus signals in a specific direction, making them ideal for point-to-point communication over long distances. They are commonly used for connecting two specific locations together.</p></p>
