<p><ul><li>Instrument Landing System CAT I</li><li>Instrument Landing System CAT II</li><li>Instrument Landing System CAT III</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Instrument Landing System (ILS) market can be categorized into three types based on the level of precision and visibility required for landing aircraft. ILS CAT I provides basic guidance for landing in conditions with a minimum visibility of 550 meters and a decision height of 200 feet. ILS CAT II offers higher precision for landing in lower visibility conditions down to 300 meters and a 100 feet decision height. ILS CAT III provides the highest level of precision and allows for landing in near zero visibility conditions.</p></p>
