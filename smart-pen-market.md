<p><ul><li>Accelerometer Based</li><li>Active Based</li><li>Positional Based</li><li>Camera Based</li><li>Trackball Pen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Accelerometer-based smart pens use motion sensors to track pen movements, providing precise data on writing speed and pressure. Active-based pens have built-in electronics for functions like data storage and wireless communication. Positional-based pens rely on sensors to determine pen location on a surface. Camera-based pens capture handwriting and convert it to digital text. Trackball pens have a trackball instead of a pen tip, allowing for more precise movement tracking. Each type offers unique features and capabilities to cater to different user needs.</p></p>
