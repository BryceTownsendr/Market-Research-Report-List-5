<p><p>Intracranial Pressure (ICP) Monitoring Devices market includes two types of devices - Invasive ICP Devices and Non-invasive ICP Devices. The market for Invasive ICP Devices is typically larger due to the higher accuracy of direct measurements, but Non-invasive ICP Devices are gaining popularity due to their less invasive nature and lower risk of complications.</p></p>
