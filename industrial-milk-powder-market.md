<p><p>Industrial milk powder market consists of two main types: whole milk powder and skim milk powder. Whole milk powder is made by evaporating liquid whole milk to remove moisture, while skim milk powder is made by removing the cream from whole milk before drying it. Whole milk powder is used in products like cheese and yogurt for its rich flavor and fat content, while skim milk powder is often used in products where a lower fat content is desired, such as protein powders and baked goods. Both types are popular in the food industry for their versatility and long shelf life.</p></p>
