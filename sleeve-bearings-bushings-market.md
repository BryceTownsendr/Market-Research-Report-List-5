<p><p>Sleeve bearings, also known as bushings, come in two main types based on their application: radial sleeve bearings and axial sleeve bearings. </p><p>Radial sleeve bearings are designed to support radial loads perpendicular to the shaft axis, while axial sleeve bearings are used to support axial loads parallel to the shaft axis. Both types of sleeve bearings are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial equipment to provide smooth and low-friction operation for rotating machinery.</p></p>
