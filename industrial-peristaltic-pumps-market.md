<p><p>Industrial Peristaltic Pumps market includes two main types - Peristaltic Tube Pumps and Peristaltic Hose Pumps. Peristaltic Tube Pumps are designed with a rotating roller that compresses a flexible tube, allowing for a gentle pumping action ideal for applications requiring low flow rates and accurate dosing. Peristaltic Hose Pumps, on the other hand, use a rotor squeezing a flexible hose to create a strong vacuum for pumping viscous fluids or slurries. Both types offer reliable and efficient pumping solutions for various industrial applications.</p></p>
