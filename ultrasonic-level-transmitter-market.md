<p><p>Ultrasonic level transmitters are commonly available in three main types: straight rod type transmitters, flange type transmitters, and screw-type transmitters. Straight rod type transmitters are ideal for applications where space is limited, while flange type transmitters are more suitable for installations requiring easy mounting. Screw-type transmitters, on the other hand, are used in situations where a compact design is necessary. Each type of transmitter offers unique advantages and is chosen based on specific installation requirements and space constraints.</p></p>
