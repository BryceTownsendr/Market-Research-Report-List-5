<p><p>Emulsion explosive sensitizers are crucial components that enhance the performance and stability of explosives. There are four main types of sensitizers used in the market: hollow glass microspheres, resin microspheres, expanded perlite, and sodium nitrite. Hollow glass microspheres are lightweight and provide excellent energy absorption. Resin microspheres offer good chemical resistance and stability. Expanded perlite is highly porous and helps control density and sensitivity. Sodium nitrite is a common oxidizing agent used for its superior stabilization properties.</p></p>
