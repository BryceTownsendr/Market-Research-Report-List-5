<p><p>Sanitary centrifugal pumps are classified based on the type of mechanical seal they use. Single mechanical seal pumps have only one seal to prevent leakage, making them simpler and more cost-effective. Double mechanical seal pumps have an additional seal for added protection against leaks, making them more secure but also more complex and expensive. The choice between single and double mechanical seal pumps depends on the specific requirements of the application, with double seal pumps providing higher levels of safety.</p></p>
