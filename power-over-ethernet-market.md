<p><p>Power over Ethernet (PoE) is a technology that allows electrical power to be transmitted over Ethernet cables, enabling networked devices such as IP cameras, VoIP phones, and wireless access points to receive power and data through a single cable. This eliminates the need for separate power cables, simplifying installation and reducing costs.</p><p>The global Power over Ethernet Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.00% during the forecast period. The increasing adoption of VoIP technology, rising demand for network security cameras, and the growing deployment of IoT devices are driving the growth of the PoE market. The ability of PoE to provide power to devices in locations where electricity may be difficult to access, such as outdoor spaces or industrial environments, is also contributing to the market's expansion.</p><p>The latest trends in the PoE market include the development of higher power PoE standards such as IEEE 802.3bt, which can deliver up to 100W of power per port. This is enabling the use of PoE in a wider range of applications, including lighting, displays, and point-of-sale terminals. Additionally, the integration of PoE with smart building technologies and the emergence of PoE-powered LED lighting systems are expected to drive further growth in the market.</p></p>
