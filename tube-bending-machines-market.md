<p><p>Tube bending machines are categorized into three main types based on their power source: fully electric, hybrid, and hydraulic. Fully electric machines use electric motors to bend tubes with precision and accuracy. Hybrid machines combine electric and hydraulic power for efficient bending. Hydraulic machines use hydraulic systems to bend tubes with high force and flexibility. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
