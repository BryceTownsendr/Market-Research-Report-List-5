<p><ul><li>Real-Time PCR</li><li>Traditional PCR</li><li>Digital PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) market is categorized into three main types: Real-Time PCR, Traditional PCR, and Digital PCR. Real-Time PCR, also known as quantitative PCR, allows for the measurement of amplification of DNA in real-time. Traditional PCR is the standard method for amplifying DNA using thermal cycling. Digital PCR is a more precise method that partitions the sample into thousands of individual reactions, enabling absolute quantification of DNA. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in various applications in research, diagnostics, and forensics.</p></p>
