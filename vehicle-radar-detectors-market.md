Full-frequency radar detectors are able to detect signals across all radar bands, providing comprehensive coverage. GPS radar detectors use GPS technology to track the vehicle's location and alert drivers of potential threats ahead. Ordinary radar detectors are more basic in functionality, while GPS full band radar detectors combine the features of GPS and full-frequency detectors for enhanced performance and accuracy.</p></p>
