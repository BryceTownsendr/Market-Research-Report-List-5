<p><p>The sucker rod market consists of three main types: steel sucker rods, FRP (Fiber Reinforced Plastic) sucker rods, and hollow sucker rods. Steel sucker rods are traditional and widely used due to their high strength and durability. FRP sucker rods are lightweight and corrosion-resistant, making them suitable for corrosive environments. Hollow sucker rods are designed for enhanced production efficiency by allowing fluid flow through the rod. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
