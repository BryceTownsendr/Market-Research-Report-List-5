<p><p>The Laser Metal Deposition Printer market can be classified into two main types: Powder Based and Wire Based. In Powder Based systems, metal powder is fed into the printer where it is melted using a high-powered laser beam to create the desired shape. On the other hand, Wire Based systems use a wire that is fed into the printer and melted to build up the metal layers. Both types of printers offer unique advantages and are used in various industries for metal additive manufacturing.</p></p>
