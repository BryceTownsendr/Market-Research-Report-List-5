<p><ul><li>X-Band Radar</li><li>S-Band Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Military coastal surveillance radar market includes X-Band Radar and S-Band Radar types. X-Band radar operates in the frequency range of 8-12 GHz, providing high resolution and accuracy for short-range surveillance. It is commonly used for maritime navigation and target tracking. S-Band radar operates in the frequency range of 2-4 GHz, offering medium-range surveillance capabilities with wider coverage. S-Band radar is suitable for long-range target detection and tracking in both military and civilian applications. Both radar types play crucial roles in enhancing coastal surveillance and security.</p></p>
