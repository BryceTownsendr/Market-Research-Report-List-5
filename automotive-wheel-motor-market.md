<p><strong>The Automotive Wheel Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner-rotor Motor</li><li>External-rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Wheel Motor Market Types include Inner-rotor Motor and External-rotor Motor. Inner-rotor Motors have the rotor positioned on the inside of the stator, providing a compact design and efficient power output. External-rotor Motors, on the other hand, have the rotor located on the outside of the stator, offering improved cooling capabilities and potentially higher torque output. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different applications based on requirements for compactness, efficiency, and cooling.</p></p>
