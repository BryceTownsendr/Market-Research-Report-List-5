<p><p>The Automotive H-Bridge Driver market consists of two main types: Single H-Bridge and Dual H-Bridge drivers. Single H-Bridge drivers are used for controlling the direction of rotation of a DC motor by reversing the polarity of the voltage applied to it. On the other hand, Dual H-Bridge drivers control the speed and direction of rotation of two DC motors independently. Both types of H-Bridge drivers are widely used in automotive applications for controlling motors in vehicles such as electric cars, hybrid vehicles, and power windows.</p></p>
