<p><p>Automotive Electronic Braking Systems consist of various types such as ABS (Anti-lock Braking System), EBD (Electronic Brakeforce Distribution), ASR (Anti-slip Regulation), ESP (Electronic Stability Program), EPB (Electronic Parking Brake), and others. ABS prevents wheels from locking during sudden braking, EBD distributes brake force to optimize stopping power, ASR helps prevent wheels from spinning on slippery surfaces, ESP enhances vehicle stability during cornering, EPB is a parking brake operated electronically, and other systems may include advanced features like adaptive cruise control.</p></p>
