<p><ul><li>Rare Earth Doping</li><li>Undoping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>YAG Transparent Ceramics market includes two main types: Rare Earth Doping and Undoping. Rare Earth Doping involves adding rare earth elements to the YAG ceramic to enhance its optical and mechanical properties, making it suitable for various applications such as laser components and optical windows. On the other hand, Undoping refers to YAG ceramics that do not contain any added rare earth elements. This type is mainly used in applications where high optical transparency and excellent mechanical strength are required without specific optical properties imparted by rare earth doping.</p></p>
