<p><p>Bicycle share markets can be classified into three main types based on their operational models. Point-to-point systems allow users to pick up and drop off bikes at any designated station. Ride-and-return systems require users to return the bike to the same station where they picked it up. Distributed markets do not have designated stations, allowing users to pick up and drop off bikes at any location within the service area. Each of these market types offers unique advantages and challenges for both users and operators.</p></p>
