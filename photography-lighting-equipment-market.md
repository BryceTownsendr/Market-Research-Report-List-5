<p><p>Photography lighting equipment market is divided into two main types - Instantaneous Lighting and Continuous Lighting. Instantaneous Lighting refers to flash strobes that provide a brief burst of light, ideal for freezing motion in fast-paced photography. On the other hand, Continuous Lighting involves a steady light source that remains on during the entire photo shoot, offering consistent illumination for video recording or long exposure photography. Both types cater to different photography needs and preferences, providing photographers with the tools to achieve desired lighting effects.</p></p>
