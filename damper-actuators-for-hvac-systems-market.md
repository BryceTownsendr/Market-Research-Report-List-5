<p><p>Damper actuators for HVAC systems are devices that control the opening and closing of dampers to regulate airflow in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. There are two main types of damper actuators in the market: Spring return damper actuators and non-spring return damper actuators. Spring return damper actuators use a spring to return the damper to its default position when the power is turned off, while non-spring return damper actuators rely on other mechanisms to control the damper position. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining indoor air quality and energy efficiency in HVAC systems.</p></p>
