<p><p>Electric & Hybrid Vehicle Driveline market comprises various types such as Series Driveline, Parallel Driveline, Power Split Driveline, Electric Driveline, and Others. Series Driveline involves the electric motor powering the wheels directly. Parallel Driveline uses both the engine and electric motor to power the wheels independently. Power Split Driveline combines engine and electric power for optimal efficiency. Electric Driveline exclusively uses electric power for propulsion. Other drivelines may include unique combinations or variations of these technologies to suit specific vehicle requirements.</p></p>
