Embedded industrial computers are well-suited for this task as they are able to operate in demanding environments while providing the necessary processing power.</p><p>Another trend impacting the market is the increasing adoption of edge computing, where data processing is performed closer to the source of data generation, rather than relying on centralized data centers.
