<p><p>Static RAM (SRAM) market can be segmented into Quad Data Rate (QDR), Double Data Rate (DDR), Asynchronous SRAM, PSRAM, and VSRAM. QDR and DDR SRAMs offer high-speed data transfer rates, making them suitable for high-performance computing applications. Asynchronous SRAM provides fast access speeds with simple control interfaces. PSRAM combines the speed of SRAM with the non-volatility of Flash memory, ideal for mobile devices. VSRAM offers high density and low power consumption, making it suitable for embedded systems and graphics applications.</p></p>
