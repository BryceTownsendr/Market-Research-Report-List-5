<p><p>There are three main types of Thin-Film Transistor-Liquid Crystal Display (TFT-LCD) markets: Twisted Nematic (TN), Vertical Alignment (VA), and In-Plane Switching (IPS). TN displays are cost-effective and offer fast response times but have limited viewing angles. VA displays provide better color reproduction and wider viewing angles than TN displays. IPS displays offer the best overall performance with accurate colors, high contrast ratios, and wide viewing angles. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences in terms of display quality and performance.</p></p>
