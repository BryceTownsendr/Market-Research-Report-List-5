<p><ul><li>Pressure-sensitive</li><li>Thermosensitive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chip capacitors can be categorized into pressure-sensitive and thermosensitive types based on their response to external stimuli. Pressure-sensitive chip capacitors change their capacitance value in response to applied pressure, making them ideal for applications where pressure variations need to be monitored. On the other hand, thermosensitive chip capacitors exhibit changes in capacitance with temperature fluctuations, making them suitable for temperature-sensitive applications. Both types offer unique functionalities and find applications in various industries such as automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
