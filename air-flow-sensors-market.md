<p><strong>The Air Flow Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mass Air Flow Sensors</li><li>Volume Air Flow Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air flow sensors are devices used to measure the volume and mass of air flowing into a vehicle's engine. Mass air flow sensors measure the actual weight of the air entering the engine, while volume air flow sensors measure the volume of air passing through. Mass air flow sensors provide more accurate measurements, while volume air flow sensors are simpler and more cost-effective. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the proper air-to-fuel ratio for efficient combustion and optimal engine performance.</p></p>
