<p><p>In the combination valve market, there are two main types: Normal Open Type and Normal Closed Type. Normal Open Type valves allow for the flow of refrigerant when there is no power supplied to the solenoid coil, while Normal Closed Type valves block the flow of refrigerant when no power is supplied. Both types are used in HVAC systems to regulate the flow of refrigerant and maintain proper temperature control. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific system requirements.</p></p>
