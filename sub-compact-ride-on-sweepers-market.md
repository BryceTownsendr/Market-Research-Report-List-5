<p><p>Sub-compact ride-on sweepers are compact cleaning machines used to clean streets, sidewalks, and other outdoor surfaces. There are three main types of sub-compact ride-on sweepers: regenerative air sweepers, mechanical broom sweepers, and vacuum street sweepers. Regenerative air sweepers use a blast of air to dislodge debris, while mechanical broom sweepers use rotating brushes to sweep up dirt. Vacuum street sweepers use suction to collect debris from the ground. Each type offers unique benefits and applications for efficient outdoor cleaning.</p></p>
