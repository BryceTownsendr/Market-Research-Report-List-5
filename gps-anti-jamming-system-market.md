<p><ul><li>Nulling</li><li>Beam Steering Systems</li><li>Civilian Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GPS Anti-Jamming Systems consists of three main types in the market - Nulling, Beam Steering Systems, and Civilian Systems. Nulling systems work by creating a signal with the same frequency as the jamming signal, but with an opposite phase, canceling out the interference. Beam Steering Systems use multiple antennas to focus on the GPS signal and ignore any jamming signals. Civilian Systems are designed for commercial use and provide protection against jamming for civilian applications. Each type offers unique benefits and functions to enhance GPS signal reliability and accuracy.</p></p>
