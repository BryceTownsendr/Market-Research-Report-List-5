<p><p>Oscillator ICs are electronic devices used to generate periodic signals in various electronic circuits. Two common types of Oscillator ICs are Fundamental Oscillator ICs and Voltage Controlled Crystal Oscillator (VCXO) ICs. Fundamental Oscillator ICs produce a stable output frequency based on the crystal used, while VCXO ICs allow for frequency modulation by applying a control voltage. Both types are essential components in communication systems, precision timing devices, and other applications requiring accurate and stable frequency generation.</p></p>
