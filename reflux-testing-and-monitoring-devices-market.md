<p><p>Reflux testing and monitoring devices market can be categorized into three main types: reflux capsule, reflux probe, and reflux catheter. Reflux capsule is a non-invasive device that is swallowed and measures pH levels in the esophagus. Reflux probe is inserted through the nose or mouth to measure pH levels in the esophagus and monitor reflux symptoms. Reflux catheter is a thin tube that is inserted through the nose or mouth to measure pH levels and assess reflux severity in the esophagus.</p></p>
