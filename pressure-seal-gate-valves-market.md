<p><p>Pressure Seal Gate Valves market types include Socket Weld Connections and Butt Weld Connections. Socket Weld Connections involve joining pipes by inserting the pipe into a recessed area of the valve, then welding the joint. Butt Weld Connections involve welding the valve directly to the pipe end. Both types of market connections provide a secure and leak-proof seal, ensuring efficient and reliable flow control in high-pressure applications. Customers can choose the type of connection based on their specific piping requirements and preferences.</p></p>
