<p><p>In the car ignition coil market, there are two main types: Single-Spark and Multi-Spark systems. Single-Spark systems use a single ignition coil to generate a spark to ignite the air-fuel mixture in the engine. On the other hand, Multi-Spark systems use multiple ignition coils to produce multiple sparks per cycle, resulting in more efficient combustion. Both systems have their own advantages and are used in different types of vehicles based on their specific requirements and performance needs.</p></p>
