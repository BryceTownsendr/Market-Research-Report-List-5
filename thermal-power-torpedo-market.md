<p><p>Thermal Power Torpedoes are primarily categorized into four market types based on their propulsion systems: Kerosene + High Pressure Air, Kerosene + Oxygen, Otto Fuel, and Others. Kerosene + High Pressure Air torpedoes use compressed air in combination with kerosene as fuel. Kerosene + Oxygen torpedoes enhance combustion efficiency by using oxygen instead of air. Otto Fuel torpedoes are powered by a specific type of fuel known as Otto fuel, which offers high energy density. Other market types include torpedoes powered by alternative fuel sources or unique propulsion systems.</p></p>
