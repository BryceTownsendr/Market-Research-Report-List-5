<p><p>Independent suspension for electric vehicles refers to a system where each wheel on the vehicle can move up and down independently, providing a smoother and more stable ride. There are various types of independent suspension systems in the market, including multi-link suspension, MacPherson strut, transverse leaf-spring, and others. Multi-link suspension uses multiple connecting rods to control wheel movement, while MacPherson strut employs a single control arm and a coil spring. Transverse leaf-spring suspension utilizes a flat spring to absorb shocks, while other systems may combine different components for optimal performance.</p></p>
