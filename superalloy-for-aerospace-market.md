<p><p>Superalloys for Aerospace are classified into three main types based on their predominant alloying elements: Fe based, Ni based, and Co based. Fe based superalloys typically offer good high-temperature strength and oxidation resistance. Ni based superalloys are known for their excellent creep and corrosion resistance at elevated temperatures. Co based superalloys are valued for their high-temperature strength, thermal stability, and resistance to wear and corrosion. Each type has unique properties that make them ideal for specific aerospace applications.</p></p>
