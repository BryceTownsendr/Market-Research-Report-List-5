<p><p>Platinum Resistance Thermometers (PRTs) are available in three different market types: 2-wire, 3-wire, and 4-wire. In a 2-wire configuration, the resistance of the connecting wires can lead to inaccuracies in temperature measurements. The 3-wire configuration helps compensate for the resistance of the wires, resulting in more accurate readings. The 4-wire configuration is the most accurate as it eliminates any errors caused by the resistance of the wires, making it ideal for high-precision temperature measurement applications.</p></p>
