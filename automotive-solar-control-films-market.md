<p><strong>The Automotive Solar Control Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Clear (Non-Reflective)</li><li>Dyed (Non-Reflective)</li><li>Vacuum Coated (Reflective)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive solar control films come in three main types: clear (non-reflective), dyed (non-reflective), and vacuum coated (reflective). Clear films allow for maximum visibility while still providing UV protection. Dyed films contain added pigments for better heat and glare reduction. Vacuum coated films have a metallic finish that reflects sunlight away from the vehicle, offering the highest level of heat rejection. Each type of film caters to different preferences and needs, providing varying levels of solar control for vehicles.</p></p>
