<p><p>Database Management Services are available in three main market types: Public Cloud, Private Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud. In the Public Cloud market, databases are hosted and managed by third-party service providers on shared servers accessible over the internet. Private Cloud databases are hosted on dedicated infrastructure for a single organization, providing increased security and control. Hybrid Cloud combines elements of both public and private clouds, allowing organizations to store sensitive data on-premises while utilizing the scalability and flexibility of the public cloud.</p></p>
