<p><p>Hermetic sliding doors come in two main types: Single leaf and double leaf. Single leaf doors consist of one sliding panel that opens to allow entry or exit, while double leaf doors have two sliding panels that open in opposite directions. Single leaf doors are commonly used in smaller spaces or where a narrower opening is needed, while double leaf doors are often used in larger areas or where a wider entryway is required. Both types of doors provide a hermetically sealed barrier, making them ideal for environments where hygiene and air-tightness are crucial.</p></p>
