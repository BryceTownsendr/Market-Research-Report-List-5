Robotic pool cleaners are autonomous machines that scrub and vacuum the pool surfaces. Suction pool cleaners operate by attaching to the pool's skimmer or suction line to collect debris. Pressure pool cleaners use the water pressure from the pool's pump to move around and clean the pool. Each type offers different features and benefits to suit various pool cleaning needs.</p></p>
