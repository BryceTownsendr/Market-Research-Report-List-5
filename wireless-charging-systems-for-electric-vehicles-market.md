<p><p>Wireless charging systems for electric vehicles come in two main types: dynamic and stationary. Dynamic wireless charging systems allow vehicles to charge while in motion, such as driving on a road embedded with charging coils. Stationary wireless charging systems, on the other hand, require vehicles to be parked over a charging pad to recharge. Both systems offer convenience and efficiency for electric vehicle owners, eliminating the need for manual plug-in charging and potentially increasing the adoption of electric vehicles.</p></p>
