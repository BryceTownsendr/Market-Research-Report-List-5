<p><p>Air jet spinning machines are categorized into three main types: Ring Spinning, Rotor Spinning, and Others. Ring spinning is the traditional method where yarn is produced by twisting fibers through a ring and traveler. Rotor spinning is a newer technology where yarn is spun by wrapping fibers around a rotor. The Others category includes machines that use different spinning techniques, such as friction spinning. Each type of machine has its advantages and is used in different industries based on specific requirements.</p></p>
