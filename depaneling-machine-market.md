<p><ul><li>In-line Depaneling Machine</li><li>Off-line Depaneling Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-line Depaneling Machines are integrated into the production line, allowing for continuous PCB depaneling without interrupting the assembly process. Off-line Depaneling Machines are standalone machines that are used separately from the production line, providing flexibility for depaneling various types of PCBs. Both types of machines offer advantages based on the specific manufacturing requirements, with in-line machines providing high-speed, automated depaneling capabilities, and off-line machines offering versatility and ease of use for smaller scale production or specialized applications.</p></p>
