<p><p>Wiped Film Evaporators (WFE) are used for the purification of heat sensitive products in various industries. There are two main types of WFE: Vertical Wiped Film Evaporators and Horizontal Wiped Film Evaporators. Vertical WFE have a vertically oriented cylindrical body, while Horizontal WFE have a horizontally oriented cylindrical body. Both types operate using a similar principle where a thin film of product is formed on the evaporator surface and is heated to evaporate volatile components, resulting in purified product.</p></p>
