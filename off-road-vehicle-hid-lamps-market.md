<p><p>Off-road vehicle HID lamps consist of two main types in the market: built-in ballast and external ballast. Built-in ballast lamps are equipped with built-in components that regulate the flow of electricity to the lamp, resulting in a more streamlined design and easier installation process. On the other hand, external ballast lamps require a separate ballast unit to regulate electricity, offering more flexibility in terms of customization and potentially higher performance levels. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and needs in the off-road vehicle market.</p></p>
