<p><p>Soldering flux paste comes in three main types: rosin-based pastes, water-soluble fluxes, and no-clean flux. Rosin-based pastes are traditional fluxes used for general soldering applications. Water-soluble fluxes are commonly chosen for their easy cleanup with water. No-clean fluxes leave behind minimal residue after soldering, eliminating the need for cleaning. Each type of flux paste offers unique advantages depending on the specific soldering requirements, making them popular choices in the market.</p></p>
