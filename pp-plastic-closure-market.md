<p><p>PP plastic closures are key components in packaging solutions for various industries. The two main market types for PP plastic closures are Compression Moulding and Injection Molding. Compression Moulding involves placing heated granular resin into a mold cavity and applying pressure to create the closure. Injection Molding, on the other hand, involves injecting molten plastic into a mold cavity to form the closure. Both methods are widely used in the production of PP plastic closures due to their efficiency and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
