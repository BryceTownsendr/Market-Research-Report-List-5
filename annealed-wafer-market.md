<p><p>Annealed wafers are used in semiconductor manufacturing. Two common types are Argon Annealed Wafer and Hydrogen Annealed Wafer. Argon annealing involves heating the wafer in an argon gas environment to remove impurities and stresses, improving the wafer's structural integrity. Hydrogen annealing, on the other hand, involves exposing the wafer to hydrogen gas to reduce defects and enhance electrical properties. Both types of annealing processes are crucial in creating high-quality wafers for use in various electronic devices.</p></p>
