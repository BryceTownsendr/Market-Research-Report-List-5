<p><p>Orthophthalic Resin Market includes two main types: Filled Resin and Unfilled Resin. Filled Resin is a type of orthophthalic resin that contains fillers such as glass fibers, calcium carbonate, or other additives to enhance properties like strength, stiffness, and thermal stability. On the other hand, Unfilled Resin is pure orthophthalic resin without any added fillers, making it more suitable for applications where high clarity or specific color is desired. Both types have their own unique properties and advantages depending on the application requirements.</p></p>
