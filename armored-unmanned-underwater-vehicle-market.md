<p><p>The Armored Unmanned Underwater Vehicle Market consists of two main types: Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROV) and Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUV). Both types of vehicles are used for a variety of applications, including underwater exploration, research, and surveillance.</p></p>
