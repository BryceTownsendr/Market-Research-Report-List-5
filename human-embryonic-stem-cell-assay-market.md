<p><p>The Human Embryonic Stem Cell Assay Market comprises various types such as Viability/Cytotoxicity, Isolation & Purification, Cell Identification, Proliferation, Differentiation, Function, and Apoptosis. Viability/Cytotoxicity assays assess the health of stem cells, while Isolation & Purification methods separate stem cells from other cell types. Cell Identification techniques determine the specific type of stem cell being studied. Proliferation assays measure the rate of stem cell growth, while Differentiation assays evaluate the ability of stem cells to mature into specialized cell types. Function assays test the functionality of stem cells, and Apoptosis assays assess cell death.</p></p>
