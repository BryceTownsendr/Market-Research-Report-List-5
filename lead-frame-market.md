<p><strong>The Lead Frame Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stamping Process Lead Frame</li><li>Etching Process Lead Frame</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lead frames are key components in semiconductor packaging, facilitating the interconnection of integrated circuits. The two main types of lead frames are produced through different manufacturing processes. Stamping process lead frames are created by stamping metal sheets to form complex shapes and designs. On the other hand, etching process lead frames are made by chemically removing unwanted metal from a sheet, resulting in intricate and precise designs. Both types cater to the diverse needs of the semiconductor industry, providing reliable and efficient solutions for packaging integrated circuits.</p></p>
