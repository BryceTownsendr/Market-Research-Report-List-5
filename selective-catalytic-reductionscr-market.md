NH3-SCR uses ammonia as the reducing agent, Urea-SCR uses urea as the reducing agent, and Ammonia-SCR directly injects ammonia into the exhaust stream. Each type of SCR system has its own advantages and disadvantages, depending on factors such as cost, efficiency, and scalability.</p></p>
