Brightfield slide scanners use white light to illuminate samples on a slide, making it suitable for imaging stained tissue samples. On the other hand, Fluorescence slide scanners utilize a specific wavelength of light to excite fluorescent molecules in the sample, enabling the visualization of specific biomarkers or molecules. Both types of scanners play a crucial role in research, diagnostics, and pathology, driving the growth of the global slide scanner market.</p></p>
