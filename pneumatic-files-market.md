<p><p>Pneumatic files can be classified into two main types based on their engine design: reciprocating engine and vane engine. Reciprocating engine pneumatic files use a piston-driven mechanism to create linear motion, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, vane engine pneumatic files utilize rotating vanes to generate rotary motion, ideal for precision tasks. Both types of pneumatic files offer different benefits and are used in various industries based on the specific needs of the task at hand.</p></p>
