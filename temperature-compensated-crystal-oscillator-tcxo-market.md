<p><p>Temperature Compensated Crystal Oscillator (TCXO) market types include surface mount mounting and thru-hole mounting. Surface mount mounting involves soldering the TCXO directly onto the surface of a PCB, while thru-hole mounting requires the TCXO to be inserted through holes on the PCB and soldered to secure it in place. Both types offer stability and precision in frequency control, with surface mount mounting being more commonly used for compact and high-density applications, while thru-hole mounting is preferred for more rugged and secure installations.</p></p>
