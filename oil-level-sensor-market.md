<p><strong>The Oil Level Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type/Point Level</li><li>Non-Contact Type/Continuous Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oil level sensors come in two main types: contact and non-contact. Contact type sensors, also known as point level sensors, detect the oil level at a specific point, providing a discrete reading of whether the oil level is above or below a certain point. Non-contact sensors, on the other hand, provide continuous level monitoring by detecting the oil level without direct contact with the liquid. Contact sensors are suitable for applications where precise level control is required, while non-contact sensors are ideal for continuous monitoring of oil levels.</p></p>
