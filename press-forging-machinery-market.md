<p><p>Press forging machinery is used in various types of forging processes such as Closed Die Forging, Open Die Forging, Extrusion, and Others. Closed Die Forging involves shaping metal between two dies, while Open Die Forging involves shaping metal without enclosing it completely. Extrusion is the process of forcing metal through a die to create a specific shape. Other types of forging include upset forging, orbital forging, and impact extrusion. Each type of forging process has its own unique benefits and applications in different industries.</p></p>
