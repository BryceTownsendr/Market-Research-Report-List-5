<p><p>The neuro endoscopic market includes two main types of endoscopes - rigid endoscopes and flexible endoscopes. Rigid endoscopes are straight and have a fixed viewing angle, making them ideal for procedures requiring precise control and stability. Flexible endoscopes have a bendable insertion tube, allowing for better access to hard-to-reach areas and a wider field of view. Both types of endoscopes are essential tools in neurosurgery, enabling minimally invasive procedures with reduced risks and faster recovery times.</p></p>
