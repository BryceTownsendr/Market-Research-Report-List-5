<p><p>Slide-staining systems are commonly categorized into two types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic systems involve minimal human intervention, with the whole staining process being performed automatically by the machine. These systems are efficient and offer high throughput capabilities, making them suitable for labs with high sample volumes. On the other hand, semi-automatic systems require some degree of human involvement, such as loading and unloading samples, while still automating the staining process. This type of system provides a balance between automation and user control.</p></p>
