Distortion effectors add grit and intensity to the sound, while overload effectors push the signal to its limits. Original sound effectors are designed to maintain the authenticity and integrity of the original sound. Delay effectors create echoes and repeats, while wow sound effectors add a dynamic and variable effect. Other markets for software effectors include modulation, filtering, reverb, and pitch shifting, each contributing unique textures and sonic qualities to the sound.</p></p>
