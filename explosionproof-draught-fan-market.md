<p><p>Explosionproof draught fans are used in hazardous environments where the risk of explosion is present. The market is divided into three main types: centrifugal, axial flow, and inclined flow. Centrifugal fans draw air into the fan housing and expel it in a different direction, making them ideal for ventilation systems. Axial flow fans move air parallel to the fan blade axis, while inclined flow fans combine both axial and centrifugal principles for efficient air movement in various applications. Each type caters to specific needs in explosionproof environments.</p></p>
