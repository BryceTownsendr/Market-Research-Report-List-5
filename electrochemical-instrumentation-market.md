<p><p>Electrochemical Instrumentation market consists of various types of instruments used in analyzing electrochemical processes. Potentiostats/Galvanostats are used to control and measure the voltage and current in electrochemical reactions. Ion Chromatographs separate and quantify ions in a sample. Conductivity Meters measure the ability of a solution to conduct electricity. Salinity Meters determine the salt concentration in a solution. Titrators are used to determine the concentration of a specific substance in a sample. pH Meters measure the acidity or alkalinity of a solution. Other instruments in this market include spectrophotometers, electrodes, and gas analyzers.</p></p>
